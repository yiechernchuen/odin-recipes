Yam Cake

Description

Yam cake is also commonly made around Chinese holidays, as a symbol of prosperity in Chinese tradition.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb taro
  • 0.50 lb regular rice flour
  • 1.5 cup water (for flour mix)
  • 0.50 oz dried shrimp
  • 1 lap cheung (chinese sausage)
  • 0.25 oz ginger (only a little bit needed)
  • 2 clove garlic
  • 1 whole piece green onion
  • 1.5 cup boiled water (for cooking taro)
  • boiled water (about 3-5 cups, enough to fill the wok until water touches the bottom of the plate)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Wash and rehydrate dried shrimp
  2. Chop ingredients
  3. Cut taro
  4. Heat wok, prepare flour
  5. Cook garlic, ginger, shrimp, lap cheung
  6. Boil water, start cooking taro
  7. Add flavors to wok
  8. Add water, cover the wok, bring to boil
  9. Coat plate with oil
  10. Uncover wok, add flour mix, sesame oil
  11. Transfer to plate, smooth out the cake
  12. Start steaming the taro cake
  13. Uncover wok, add garnish, and cool
  14. Slice the taro cake, pan fry
